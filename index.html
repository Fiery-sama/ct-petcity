About Us 🐾

At Pet-Friendly City, we believe that kindness shouldn't stop at humans. Our mission is to create safer, more compassionate urban spaces for stray animals — one city at a time.

We're a youth-led movement turning empathy into action. From rescue and rehabilitation to fostering and awareness drives, our volunteers are the heartbeat of change on the streets. Whether it's feeding a hungry pup, helping with adoptions, or organizing pet-health camps — every effort counts.

Our goal?
To build cities where no paw is left behind — where strays are not ignored, but embraced, protected, and loved.

Join us in making every street a little more wag-worthy.
Because when a city becomes friendly for animals, it becomes more humane for all.
